Strategy Builder

What is this? A plain-English trading idea saved locally. Why it matters: paper trading needs a strategy before it can simulate. What to do next: fill the fields or use the safe paper example, then click Save Strategy.

Use a plain label you will recognize later, such as Polygon momentum pullback.

Use the pair you want to study. Paper trading uses local market data, not money.

Shorter timeframes create more signals; longer timeframes are slower and usually easier to review.

Use structured arbitrage fields for multi-venue strategies. EtherealAI will not try to parse arbitrage from a paragraph.
